Leslie Parry

Author with her debut novel being published in the U.S. by Ecco and in the U.K. by Two Roads Books.

Problem:Leslie's first novel, House of Marvels, is being released in May. The author needed a web presence that evoked the feeling of her story and that she could easily edit herself.

Solution: Created a design that uses subtle elements from the first book. This design can easily evolve to represent Leslie specific style after her second (and third) books come out without losing focus on the original look. Researched and assembled a series of public domain images within the era of House of Marvels for Leslie to use on the site and her blog. Squarespace was utilized in order for Leslie to easily add events and edit purchase information.
