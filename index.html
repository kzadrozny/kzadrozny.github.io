About

Kathy Zadrozny is a freelance graphic designer, co-founder of the Letter Writers Alliance, and librarian living in Chicago. She started her design career working in advertising, before she got her masters in Information Science, and now specializes in brand identity and web design.

For the past four years she has been teaching and organizing workshops at Chicago Public Library, Forest Park Public LIbrary, Read/Write Library, and online platforms such as Google On Air. Kathy's projects and courses have been successful enough to garner attention from BBC World Service, WBEZ (NPR), RedEye, and Gapers Block. Her work has also been featured in the books Good Mail Day, Craft Business Heroes, and she wrote the foreword to the upcoming book Mary & Me: A Lasting Link Through Ink.
